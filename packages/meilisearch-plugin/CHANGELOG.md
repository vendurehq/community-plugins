## 1.0.0

Initial release as `@vendure-community/meilisearch-plugin`.

### Features

* **meilisearch-plugin:** Meilisearch-powered product search as a drop-in replacement for DefaultSearchPlugin
* **meilisearch-plugin:** AI hybrid search support with configurable embedders
* **meilisearch-plugin:** similar documents query using AI embeddings
* **meilisearch-plugin:** typo tolerance, synonyms, stop words configuration
* **meilisearch-plugin:** custom product and variant mappings
* **meilisearch-plugin:** price range filtering and price bucket aggregation
* **meilisearch-plugin:** in-stock filtering with stock movement event handling
* **meilisearch-plugin:** zero-downtime reindexing via atomic index swaps
